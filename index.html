My background

University of Science

I finished my Computer Science studies and earned a title of an Engineer at the end of January 2022. I graduated from Wrocław University of Science and Technology where I studied for over 3 years. During that time I found out that programming can be really entertaining and it can change our lives for better. This is why I would like to continue this journey and learn even more.

Software Engineer

I got interested in software engineering during my studies. Before I learned about the role of a Software Engineer I thought programming is just writing code and hoping it will run as planned. The craftmanship of a good Software Enginner ensures that projects will be finished on time and the quality of the provided solution will be more than just acceptable. My goal is to constantly hone my skills as a Software Enginner and be better at the craft.

Fullstack Developer

When I was working in group projects I was always the one responsible for the backend of the application but I always felt like this is not enough. I did not like being so strongly dependent on other people and not being able to help them. This is why I got interested in learning more about the frontend technologies (mainly related to Javascript) to become a true fullstack developer in the future.
